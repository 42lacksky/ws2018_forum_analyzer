About our project

Find Idea it is the application for the analysis of the users experience on the basis of correspondence in publics of the organizations.

It allows to reduce time for identification of merits and demerits both at own products and at competitors

Help

How to use the service "FIND IDEA":

1. Enter the link of analysable discussion of the VKontakte group and press the "GO" button;

2. If you want to look through at the list of all earlier analyzed groups and choose one of them, follow the link in the bottom of the "Home" page;

3. Choose the way of representation comments: as TAGS - the most popular words or as CLUSTERS - the main themes correspondence;

4. On the next stap you will see the list of TAGS or CLUSTERS. If you are on the cluster's page, you can change the count of clusters by pressing button with number;

5. If you see the list for particular tag or cluster, you can choose the comment and look through information about it.
